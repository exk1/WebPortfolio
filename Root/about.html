About Me

Welcome to my design portfolio.

My name is Emma Krebs and I am a senior at the University of Pittsburgh studying Digital Narrative and Interactive Design as well as Political Science. My major encompasses the disciplines of media creation, narrative writing, and programming. Some relevant courses I have taken include Visual Literacy, Integrating Writing and Design, and Composing Digital Media Through these topics I have learned about digital media and visual culture in our contemporary world and how we interpret it. As well as how to bring communicative and modernized media to the professional world.

For the past year I have been fortunate to be the Communications Intern for my University's Office of Sustainability. I have excelled as a designer to create both informative and visually exciting graphics and content throughout my time in this role. I have worked extensively with content creation tools and content management tools such as Adobe Suite, Canva, WordPress, and social media platforms to create relevant brand campaigns.

In my spare time I am the Communications Director for the Amnesty International Chapter at my University. As the Communication Director, I am committed to creating communicative strategies that connect audiences to our organization's mission of advocating for human rights and creating positive change.

I have aspirations to further my professional experience with creative engagement strategies. As well as, intersecting my interests of design, media, and policy which I hope to utilize in my future careers and as I finish my degree.
